Chapter 93: The Morning Sunlight

By the morning sunlight. and the night when it falls still! Your Lord has not abandoned you, nor has He become hateful 'of you'. And the next life is certainly far better for you than this one. And 'surely' your Lord will give so much to you that you will be pleased. Did he not find you as an orphan then sheltered you? Did He not find you unguided then guided you? And did He not find you needy then satisied your needs? So do not oppress the orphan, nor repulse the beggar. And proclaim the blessings of your Lord.
